About Me

My name is Djouher Otmani. I am Kabyle from Algeria, originally from Tizi Ouzou, and I am currently living in Montreal. I am studying at Algonquin College in the Media Design program, where I am learning the basics of web development. Through this course, I am discovering how HTML works and why structure and meaning are important in web pages. I really enjoy learning new digital skills and improving step by step through practice and patience.

Moving to Canada has been a meaningful experience for me, both personally and academically. This program helps me build confidence in creating websites and understanding semantic elements. I am motivated to continue learning and growing in this field because technology plays a big role in creativity today. This is my 1st semester building a complete HTML page from scratch, and it has been a very positive learning experience.
